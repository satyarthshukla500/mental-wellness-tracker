Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely! We take your privacy seriously. All your wellness data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent.

I need urgent mental health support

If you're experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact emergency services immediately. For immediate support, call your local crisis hotline or 988 (US National Suicide Prevention Lifeline).

How do I use the app features?

Our app is designed to be intuitive! Start by adding your first mood entry on the Dashboard. Use the Reminders section to set up daily check-ins, and explore your Profile for personalized insights.

Can I export my wellness data?

Yes! You can export all your wellness entries as a CSV file from your Dashboard. This allows you to keep a backup of your data or share it with healthcare providers if needed.

Important Safety Information

If you're experiencing a mental health emergency in India:

  • Call 112 (India's National Emergency Helpline)
  • Contact the AASRA Helpline for suicide prevention: 91-9820466726
  • Call 9152987821 for the Vandrevala Foundation Mental Health Helpline
  • Contact the Snehi Emotional Support Helpline: 91-9582208181
  • Call 1091 for Women's Helpline or 1098 for Child Helpline
  • Visit your nearest emergency room if needed

Remember: MindSpace is a wellness tracking tool, not a replacement for professional mental health care. If you're struggling with your mental health, please reach out to a qualified mental health professional.
